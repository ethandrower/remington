# SLA Escalation Thresholds

## Level 1: Soft Reminder (2 days overdue)
- Friendly Jira comment to developer

## Level 2: Jira + Slack (3 days overdue)
- Jira comment + Slack thread visibility

## Level 3: Team Escalation (5 days overdue)
- Developer + Tech Lead notification

## Level 4: Leadership (7+ days overdue)
- Management + stakeholder escalation
